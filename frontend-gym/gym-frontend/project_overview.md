# Project Overview

# SaaS Gym Management System

## Introduction

The SaaS Gym Management System is a fully customized, multi-tenant software platform designed to help gym owners efficiently manage their entire business from a centralized dashboard. The system provides comprehensive tools for member management, attendance tracking, health monitoring, CRM operations, staff management, communication automation, and business reporting.

The platform is designed as a scalable SaaS solution, allowing multiple gyms and branches to operate independently while being managed through a centralized Super Admin panel. The architecture is future-ready and supports additional modules and integrations as business requirements evolve.

---

# Project Vision

To provide gym owners with a complete digital ecosystem that automates daily operations, improves member engagement, enhances staff productivity, and increases business growth through data-driven management and communication tools.

---

# Project Goals

* Digitize gym operations and member management.
* Simplify attendance and membership tracking.
* Improve lead conversion through integrated CRM tools.
* Enable health monitoring and fitness tracking for members.
* Automate communication using Email and WhatsApp integrations.
* Support multiple gyms and branches through SaaS architecture.
* Provide scalable infrastructure for future expansion.

---

# Target Users

## Super Admin

Responsible for managing the entire SaaS platform including gyms, subscriptions, users, reports, and system-wide configurations.

## Gym Owner

Responsible for managing gym operations, memberships, staff, attendance, and reports for their organization.

## Admin

Handles day-to-day operational activities within the gym.

## Trainer

Manages member fitness activities, food charts, BMI tracking, and health monitoring.

## Sales Staff

Responsible for lead management, follow-ups, and membership conversions.

## Customer / Member

Accesses personal fitness information, attendance records, memberships, food charts, and notifications.
